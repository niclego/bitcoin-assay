Methodology & honesty
BTC price and market cap fetch live from CoinGecko (keyless). Gold and silver caps = spot price × fixed above-ground stock (~6.95 B oz gold, ~56.3 B oz silver); spot comes from a fallback chain — gold-api.com, then goldprice.org, then tokenized-metal proxies (PAXG/KAG) on CoinGecko, which track spot within ~1%. Long-term-holder supply (155-day definition) auto-loads from bitcoin-data.com (BGeometrics); it reproduces the UTXO-age trend, not Glassnode's proprietary entity-adjusted level — read its direction, not its exact level. Last-good values are cached on this device and used, labelled, when a feed is down. Waypoints: 0.05 was the 2017 floor, ~0.15 the 2021 and 2024 peaks, 0.20–0.30 the convergence target. Not investment advice — an instrument for one long thesis you already hold.
